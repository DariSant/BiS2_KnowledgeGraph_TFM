Also, we have investigated the variation of Tc at different applied pressures using four probe techniques. The Tc onset decreases gradually from 4.8 K to 4.3 K in Bi4O4S3 for all the applied pressures (0 to 1.4 GPa) and therefore it holds a negative pressure coefficient of -0.287 K/GPa. Contrarily, the pressure effect on Tc of NdO0.5F0.5BiS2 has positive pressure coefficient where the Tc onset increases up to 1.31 GPa at a rate of 0.435 K/GPa and thereafter it reduces for further enhancement of pressure becomes negative coefficient (dTc onset/dP= -0.1714 K/GPa K/GPa) on Tc at high pressure of 1.75 GPa.
